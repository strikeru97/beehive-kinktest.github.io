For each item, please select the level that shares the closest correspondence between your feelings and the topic. After you finish, click the export button, enter an identifier of your choosing, and then press okay. A link will be generated that contains a picture of your answers.

Rating descriptions:

- Favorite: You are excited to partake in the specified activity, and/or you are extremely attracted to this feature/behavior.

- Like: You enjoy the specified activity, and/or you are attracted to this feature/behavior.

- Okay: You are willing to partake in the specified activity, and/or you are somewhat attracted of this particular feature/behavior.

- Maybe: Depending on the circumstances, you might be willing to partake in the specified activity, and/or you might be attracted to this particular feature/behavior.

- Probably Not: You are likely unwilling to partake in the specified activity, and/or you are unlikely to be attracted to this particular feature/behavior.

- Limit: You are not willing to participate in the specified activity, and/or you are not attracted of this particular feature/behavior.

- Unknown: You do not know enough about this particular activity, feature, or behavior to make a judgment on it.

- Not Applicable: It is impossible for you to partake in this specific activity, or be attracted to this feature/behavior. For example, you are a male, so you are unable to become pregnant. Thus, you cannot enjoy being impregnated. For another example, you are deaf, so it is impossible for you to be attracted to auditory stimulation.
